# Templates

This directory contains official document and presentation templates for kieks.me GbR.

## Available Templates

### Document Templates
- Business letters
- Invoices
- Proposals
- Reports
- Contracts

### Presentation Templates
- PowerPoint/Keynote presentations
- Google Slides templates
- Pitch decks

### Marketing Templates
- Brochures
- Flyers
- Business cards
- Email signatures
- Social media graphics

### Digital Templates
- Website mockups
- Email templates
- Newsletter layouts

## File Formats

Templates should be provided in multiple formats when possible:
- Microsoft Office (DOCX, PPTX)
- LibreOffice/OpenOffice (ODT, ODP)
- Adobe Creative Suite (INDD, AI, PSD)
- Google Workspace compatible formats
- PDF (for reference)

## Usage

1. Download the appropriate template for your needs
2. Make a copy before editing (never modify the original)
3. Replace placeholder content with your specific information
4. Maintain the layout, colors, and fonts as specified
5. Refer to brand guidelines for proper usage
