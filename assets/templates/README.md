# Templates

This directory contains brand-compliant templates for various applications.

## Available Templates

### Document Templates
- **Letterhead**: For official correspondence
- **Business cards**: Contact card designs
- **Invoices**: Branded invoice templates
- **Proposals**: Proposal document templates
- **Reports**: Report cover and layout templates

### Presentation Templates
- **PowerPoint/Keynote**: Slide deck templates
- **Google Slides**: Cloud-based presentation templates

### Digital Templates
- **Email signatures**: HTML email signature templates
- **Social media**: Post templates for various platforms
- **Newsletter**: Email newsletter templates

### Print Templates
- **Brochures**: Tri-fold and bi-fold designs
- **Flyers**: Event and promotional flyers
- **Posters**: Large format poster templates

## File Formats

Templates are provided in various formats depending on the use case:
- **.pptx** / **.key**: Presentation templates
- **.docx** / **.pages**: Document templates
- **.indd**: InDesign print templates
- **.pdf**: Read-only reference and print-ready files
- **.html**: Email and web templates

## Using Templates

1. **Download** the appropriate template
2. **Duplicate** the file (don't edit the original)
3. **Customize** with your content
4. **Follow** brand guidelines for colors, fonts, and logo usage
5. **Save** your final version

## Template Guidelines

### Do's
✓ Use templates as a starting point  
✓ Maintain the established grid and layout  
✓ Use approved brand colors and fonts  
✓ Keep logo placement consistent  
✓ Adapt templates for your specific needs while maintaining brand consistency

### Don'ts
✗ Don't dramatically alter the template layout  
✗ Don't use non-brand colors or fonts  
✗ Don't remove or modify logo clear space  
✗ Don't overcrowd the design  
✗ Don't use low-resolution images

## Customization

While templates provide a starting point, some customization is expected:
- Replace placeholder text with your content
- Swap placeholder images with relevant photos
- Adjust content length and layout as needed
- Maintain overall brand aesthetic

## Requesting New Templates

Need a template that doesn't exist yet? Please contact the brand team with:
- Template purpose and use case
- Preferred format
- Any specific requirements
- Timeline for completion

## Contributing Templates

When adding new templates:
1. Ensure compliance with brand guidelines
2. Use proper naming conventions
3. Include a preview/thumbnail image
4. Document any special instructions
5. Test the template before adding to repository
