Corporate Identity & Corporate Design

Brand assets, guidelines, and resources for kieks.me GbR

About This Repository

This repository serves as the central source of truth for all Corporate Identity (CI) and Corporate Design (CD) materials for kieks.me GbR. It contains brand assets, guidelines, templates, and resources to ensure consistent brand representation across all company communications and materials.

All company members are allowed and encouraged to use these materials within the company context. Use logos, colors, fonts, and templates for company projects, reference brand guidelines for consistent communications, and download assets for internal and external company materials.
