Email Footer

Professional email footer templates following brand guidelines

Brand Guidelines • Email Compatible • Responsive Design

Overview

Our email footer templates are designed to follow kieks.me brand fundamentals, ensuring consistency across all email communications. Each footer features our brand colors, typography, and logo, optimized for maximum compatibility with email clients.

Design Features

  • Brand logo (kieks.me horizontal variant)
  • Contact information (email, phone, website)
  • Company name and legal information
  • Social media links (optional)
  • Navy and aqua color scheme

Technical Features

  • Table-based layout for email compatibility
  • Inline CSS styles (no external stylesheets)
  • Responsive design for mobile devices
  • WCAG AA compliant color contrast
  • Compatible with major email clients

Design Features

Brand Colors

Uses our official brand colors: Navy (#1E2A45) with Aqua accents (#00FFDC) for optimal contrast and brand recognition in email clients.

Typography

Follows brand typography guidelines: Hanken Grotesk for headings and Source Sans 3 for body text, with web-safe fallbacks for email compatibility.

Email Compatibility

Built with table-based layouts and inline styles for maximum compatibility with Gmail, Outlook, Apple Mail, and other major email clients.

Technical Details

Email Client Compatibility

Our email footer templates are designed to work across all major email clients:

  • Gmail (Web, iOS, Android)
  • Outlook (Windows, Mac, Web)
  • Apple Mail (macOS, iOS)
  • Yahoo Mail
  • Thunderbird
  • And many more...

Best Practices

When using these email footer templates:

  • Always use inline styles (no external CSS files)
  • Use table-based layouts for structure
  • Test in multiple email clients before sending
  • Keep image file sizes small for faster loading
  • Use absolute URLs for images and links
  • Ensure all text has sufficient color contrast (WCAG AA)
