Business Cards

Professional business cards with QR code integration

Brand Guidelines • QR Code • vCard Integration

Overview

Our business cards are designed following kieks.me brand fundamentals, ensuring consistency across all brand touchpoints. Each card features our brand colors, typography, and logo, combined with modern QR code technology for seamless contact sharing.

Front Side

  • Brand logo (kieks.me single circle variant)
  • Contact information (name, position, email, phone)
  • Company name (kieks.me GbR)
  • Navy background with aqua accents

Back Side

  • QR code containing vCard (VCF) data
  • Scannable contact information
  • Direct import into contact apps
  • Clean, minimal design

Design Features

Brand Colors

Uses our official brand colors: Navy background (#1E2A45) with Aqua accents (#00FFDC) for optimal contrast and brand recognition.

Typography

Follows brand typography guidelines: Hanken Grotesk for headings and Source Sans 3 for body text, ensuring readability and consistency.

Standard Format

Standard business card size: 85mm × 55mm (ISO/IEC 7810 ID-1 format), compatible with standard card holders and printing services.

Technical Details

QR Code Integration

Each business card includes a QR code on the back that contains vCard (VCF) formatted contact data. When scanned, the QR code allows users to:

  • Import contact information directly into their phone's contacts
  • Access contact details without manual data entry
  • Share contact information digitally

Template System

Business cards are generated using HTML/CSS templates that follow our brand guidelines. The templates support:

  • Dynamic content insertion
  • Conditional rendering of optional fields
  • PDF generation via Puppeteer
  • Consistent brand styling
